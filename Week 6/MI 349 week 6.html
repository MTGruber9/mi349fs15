MI 349 Week 6

Chunking Example

Chunking: Combining information into a limited amount of chunks for easier processing and recall.


Bad Example

No chunking
Employee ID #: 650123
Phone Number: 1238904567
Date of Birth: 03081993
Login Code: B3D44yZ99

Good Example

With chunking
Employee ID #: 650-123
Phone Number: 1(238) 622-9045
Date of Birth: 07 / 08 / 1991
Login Code: B3G4-7XZ9-92
